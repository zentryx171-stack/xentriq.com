Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a website?

Typically, a standard website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms may take 6-8 weeks. The timeline depends on the project scope and requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support to ensure your digital assets run smoothly.

Can you help with mobile app development for both iOS and Android?

Absolutely! We develop native apps for both iOS and Android, as well as cross-platform solutions using React Native and Flutter for cost-effective deployment.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on the complexity, features, and timeline. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration and can accommodate different time zones and communication preferences.
